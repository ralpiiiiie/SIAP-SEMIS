BUSINESS CHALLENGE

Understanding the issues faced by Chef Alex’s Dinuguan Station in managing daily operations.

Chef Alex’s Dinuguan Station currently uses a paper-based, manual system to record orders, payments, and delivery schedules.

This causes missed or late deliveries, difficulty tracking partial or pending payments, frequent calculation errors in daily/monthly sales, and slow retrieval of customer records.

Without automated reminders or centralized records, staff spend extra time correcting mistakes and the owner cannot quickly analyze sales trends or make timely decisions.

PROPOSED IT SOLUTION

Implementing a spreadsheet-based system for faster and more accurate order, payment, and delivery tracking.

To address the issues caused by manual recording, our group created an Automated Order, Payment, and Delivery Tracking System using spreadsheets.

This system automatically updates records, calculates totals, monitors due dates, and highlights payment and delivery statuses.

It helps streamline operations, minimize human error, and improve efficiency in managing customer orders and business transactions.

THE IMPACT OF OUR IT SOLUTION

How Chef Alex’s Dinuguan Station benefits from digital transformation.

After implementing the Automated Order, Payment, and Delivery Tracking System, Chef Alex’s Dinuguan Station will have a more efficient and organized process.

The system will speed up order recording, reduce errors in payments and deliveries, and ensure timely customer service.

With automated updates and clear reports, the business can save time, improve accuracy, and focus on serving more customers effectively.
